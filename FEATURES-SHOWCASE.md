## 🎯 Feature Demonstrations

### 1. AI Companion (★★★★★)

**What to Show**:
```
1. Click "AI Companion" from dashboard
2. Type: "I'm feeling lonely today"
3. Show AI's empathetic response
4. Click microphone icon
5. Speak: "Tell me a story"
6. Show voice-to-text conversion
7. Listen to AI's text-to-speech response
```

**Key Points to Mention**:
- GPT-3.5 Turbo integration
- Custom prompts for elderly users
- Context-aware conversations
- Voice input/output
- Fallback responses for reliability

**Wow Factor**: 
> "The AI remembers previous conversations and adapts its responses based on the user's emotional state."

### 2. Medicine Reminders (★★★★★)

**What to Show**:
```
1. Click "Medicine Reminders"
2. Click "Add New Medicine"
3. Fill in:
   - Name: "Aspirin"
   - Dosage: "1 tablet"
   - Times: "09:00, 14:00, 21:00"
   - With food: ✓
4. Save medicine
5. Show upcoming reminders on dashboard
6. (If time allows) Wait for notification
```

**Key Points to Mention**:
- Multiple daily reminders
- Email + browser notifications
- Adherence tracking
- "With food" indicators
- Family notification system

**Wow Factor**:
> "The system sends reminders via email, SMS, and browser notifications - ensuring users never miss a dose."

### 3. Social Health Tracking (★★★★☆)

**What to Show**:
```
1. Click "Daily Check-in" from dashboard
2. Answer 5 questions:
   - How are you feeling? → "Wonderful"
   - Meaningful conversation? → "Yes, several"
   - Spoke with family? → "Yes, had a good chat"
   - Felt useful? → "Yes, definitely"
   - How connected? → "Very connected"
3. Show calculated score (e.g., 85/100)
4. View weekly trend graph
5. Show personalized recommendations
```

**Key Points to Mention**:
- Quantifiable metric (0-100)
- Based on 5 daily questions
- Weekly trend visualization
- Personalized recommendations
- Early intervention triggers

**Wow Factor**:
> "This is the first app to quantify social isolation with a scientific, data-driven approach."
